Aortic Sclerosis

Aortic Area Murmur
[2nd Intercostal space on the right]
Systolic

 

  • May mimic aortic stenosis but is not associated with significant stenosis.
  • Is secondary to calcific deposits on the aortic annulus with or without invasion onto the leaflets themselves.
  • Usually does not invade the second heart sound.
  • May be associated with aortic insufficiency.
  • Most commonly found in the elderly population.
