Patent Ductus Arteriosus

Pulmonic Area Murmur
[2nd Intercostal space on the right]
Systolic

 

Advanced PDA

  • May be silent when small.
  • Moderately small to large lesions give a continuous (systolic and diastolic) murmur heard throughout the precordium.
  • Large lesions associated with a wide pulse pressure.
  • If severe pulmonary hypertension occurs, Eisenmenger’s physiology may be present with disappearance of the murmur and differential cyanosis.
